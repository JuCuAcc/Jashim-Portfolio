About me

Hi, my name is MD. Jashim Uddin, and I am a dedicated software enginneer based in Bangladesh. I have a strong passion for technology and a drive to create innovative solutions. With a background in business administration, I completed my MBA and BBA from the University of Chittagong in Chattogram, Bangladesh.

Since professional training in IDB-BISEW, I have been deeply involved in studying programming, both within my academic curriculum and through self-learning via various online platforms. I have explored a wide range of resources, including video tutorials, interactive websites like freeCodeCamp and Codecademy, and mobile apps such as Sololearn. Additionally, I have completed more than 10 courses from Coursera and Udemy.

Over time, I have accumulated extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, C#, T-SQL, ASP.NET MVC,.NET Core, Angular, Flutter, Web API, and Git. I have a strong work ethic and consider myself a workaholic, constantly striving for growth and improvement. I see programming as my future career and truly enjoy the process of creating software solutions. It's like having superpowers in real life, and as a bonus, I have a love for teaching that I cultivated during my younger years.

I am currently seeking a senior software enginneer position where I can apply my existing skills and continue to grow in a professional environment. With my dedication and passion for programming, I am confident in my ability to contribute to exciting projects and make a positive impact.
